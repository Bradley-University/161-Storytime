Let's write a story

Write a story about a person who discovers a mysterious object in their backyard. What is the object, and how does it affect the person's life? What secrets and mysteries does it hold? (Add your name at the end of the paragraph)

We open on a lone house in the middle of the countryside, with nothing but the lone two-story building with flaking paint off the side of the house. With the closest civilization being the convenience store a mile or so away, young Isaac has had a pretty boring life to this point. That is until he is startled awake early in the morning by the sound of a large "BOOM!" from his backyard! - Caden Sheahan

The explosion shook his house- taking off what little paint was left. His ears still ringing, Isaac peered out his window to find a crater in his backyard. A large stone sitting in the middle of the rubble. Clearly some kind of meteor had struck- but from where? - Andrew Czarnik

Unsure how to proceed, Isaac slowly crept downstairs, wanting to get a closer look at this meteor. As he stepped outside, he noticed that it began to glow. Isaac froze place, transfixed by the meteor. - Alex Chartrand
